What I do

UI/UX Design

Specializing in creating intuitive and user-friendly digital interfaces, employing user-centered design principles to improve user satisfaction and engagement.

Graphic Design

Crafting visually compelling designs with a mastery in color theory, typography, and Gestalt principles, focusing on branding and identity across all touchpoints.

Interaction Design

Developing interactive elements that enhance the user journey, using motion and behavior design to guide and inform users about the elements.

Accessibility

Conducting WCAG 2.1 compliance audits and implementing accessible design practices to make digital products usable by as many people as possible.

Coding

Leveraging front-end development skills for effective collaboration with developers to ensure designs are feasible and implemented with fidelity.

User Research

Utilizing a variety of research methods to gather insights into user needs & behaviors, informing design decisions and ensuring products meet user expectations.
